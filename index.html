📐 Risk Scoring Methodology

🌰 How We Score Bridge Risk

Risk scores (0–100) combine three weighted factors reflecting real-world attack economics.

💰 TVL / Volume Exposure (0–40 pts)

Higher volume = more attractive target. Bridges processing >$1B weekly score maximum. Historical data shows exploit size correlates strongly with available TVL.

💀 Exploit History (0–40 pts)

Bridges with prior exploits receive a 40-point penalty. Attackers target previously compromised protocols — they know the architecture and residual vulnerabilities.

🌐 Chain Surface Area (0–20 pts)

Each chain integration adds smart contract risk, relayer trust assumptions, and validation gaps. Bridges on 10+ chains score maximum surface-area risk.

📊 Thresholds

CRITICAL ≥ 70 pts
HIGH 50–69 pts
MEDIUM 30–49 pts
LOW < 30 pts
